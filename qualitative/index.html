Suzie's World

from Fabrics to Fashion


A journey through the original watercolor and gouache design drawings by the legendary and prolific textile designer Suzie Zuzek (1920 - 2011). Between 1962 and 1985, Zuzek created over 1,500 designs for Key West Hand Print Fabrics in Key West, Florida, which were used by Lilly Pulitzer. Via the amazing collections from the Cooper Hewitt Smithsonian Design Museumhis website aims to reveal the nature and scope of Zuzek's artistic contribution to the iconic Pulitzer style.
